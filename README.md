## Project Overview
An end-to-end Tableau case study analyzing Airbnb-style short-term rental data 
for San Diego. The project includes an interactive dashboard and a 9-slide 
Tableau Story with narrative business insights.

## Key Insights
- **Top earner:** PB Beach House at $113,772 — beachfront + high minimum nights = max revenue
- **Sweet spot pricing:** The $301–500 bucket generates the most total earnings — not the luxury tier
- **Location matters:** Shore properties (Pacific Beach, Mission Beach, La Jolla) account for the majority of earnings vs. inland
- **Reviews vs. ratings:** Budget listings ($0–50) attract the most reviews, but review scores stay stable at 94–96 across all price tiers
- **Seasonality:** Average daily price peaks in July ($334) and August ($317) — only 2 months cross the $300 threshold
- **Room type:** Entire home/apt dominates with 8,304 listings (70%) and holds 83% of all beds
- **Top host:** Michael leads at $515K — moderate pricing + high bookings wins over premium pricing

## Dashboard Features
- Top 10 Earners bar chart
- Price Bucket vs. Earnings analysis
- Shore vs. Inland earnings comparison
- Neighbourhood-level breakdown
- Price vs. Reviews & Ratings (dual-axis)
- 9-slide Tableau Story with business narrative captions
